Our mission

Offering newer and nicer vehicles with newer and nicer budgets.

Municipal Fleet Services emerged to fill the void in the market between the manufacturers and the municipal customer. We put our customer's needs first, and since 2014, our program has seen unprecedented growth in the market.

In fact, our program was created in cooperation with local government administration, fleet managers, state attorneys, and dealers to provide a simple, straightforward lease that eliminates many of the hassles typically associated with municipal financing.

  • We provide real solutions for the complex world of municipal finance.
  • We specialize in municipal fleet services of all types;
    Purchases, Leases, Up-fits, Tracking Technology, Service and Maintenance, and Budget Projections.
